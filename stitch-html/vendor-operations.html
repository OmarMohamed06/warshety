Pricing & Commission Agreement

1. Commission Structure

The Marketplace shall charge a standard commission fee of 12% on all completed part sales and 8% on service bookings made through the platform. Fees are calculated on the gross transaction value inclusive of VAT.

2. Payout Terms

Payouts to Egyptian bank accounts will be processed every Tuesday for all transactions completed and confirmed during the previous calendar week (Monday to Sunday).

3. Vendor Obligations

Vendors must maintain accurate pricing that matches their in-store or offline prices. Marketplace customers should not be charged more than walk-in customers for the same items or services.

4. Quality Guarantee

Vendors agree to provide genuine parts and professional services. Repeated complaints regarding part authenticity or service quality may lead to account suspension.
